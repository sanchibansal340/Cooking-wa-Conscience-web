About Us

What are we?

We are a 501(c)3 organization that provides sustainable, easy, and delicious cooking classes to young girls.

Motivation

It all started when we saw the lack of culinary education that young girls had and we had the idea to change it.

What do we do?

In our classes, we teach young girls how to prepare delicious recipes that also happen to be very environmentally conscious.

Join Us

Are you a Social Bird?

We are currently looking for people to work in Social Media Management, who would help us time posts, and expand our social media across more platforms.

Or are you a Digital Queen?

We are also looking for an Ambassador, whose role it would be to endorse our posts/events to other people.

Or do you have awesome leadership skills?

We are looking for a chapter leader who would run cooking classes in her area.

We would send you the ingredients and recipes, but it would be your responsibility to find a group of girls to teach (such as setting up a club at your local elementary school).

If any of this feels like you, then don't forget to apply below!
