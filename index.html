About Us

What are we?

We are a 501(c)3 organization that provides sustainable, easy, and delicious cooking classes to young girls.

Motivation

It all started when we saw the lack of culinary education that young girls had and we had the idea to change it.

What do we do?

In our classes, we teach young girls how to prepare delicious recipes that also happen to be very environmentally conscious.
